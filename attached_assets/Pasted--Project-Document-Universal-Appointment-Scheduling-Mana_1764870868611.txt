1. Project Overview

The Universal Appointment & Scheduling Management System is designed to streamline booking, managing, and tracking appointments across various professions such as doctors, salons, lawyers, consultants, therapists, tutors, and more. The system provides an organized, user-friendly workflow to ensure professionals can manage their time efficiently, while clients can easily schedule appointments within available time slots.

2. Objectives
Primary Objectives

Enable professionals to define their working hours and available time slots.

Allow clients to book appointments within permitted schedules.

Prevent overlapping or double-booked appointments.

Provide reminders and notifications to both parties.

Offer a clean dashboard for viewing and managing upcoming appointments.

Secondary Objectives

Maintain records of past, cancelled, and completed appointments.

Provide professionals the ability to block or edit time schedules.

Support multiple categories of professionals with unique scheduling needs.

Ensure smooth communication between professionals and clients.
